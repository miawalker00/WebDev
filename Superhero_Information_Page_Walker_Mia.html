Backstory

Black Widow, or Natasha Romanoff, was born in the Soviet Union.
Romanoff's skills were developed when she was young, after being
adopted and trained by Ivan Petrovitch Bezukhov. Later in life,
her skills were recognized by the KGB. She was brainwashed into
becoming a master assassin by The Hand. Romanoff trained in the Black
Widow Program, where young girls were trained to become sleeper
agents. The KGB also injected Romanoff with a Super-Soldier serum.
Later, she was rescued the well known Captain America and Wolverine.
Romanoff eventually left the KGB to fight for The Avengers.
